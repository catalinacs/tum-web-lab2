We are a family-run bakery focused on simple, high-quality ingredients and joyful flavors. We bake small batches to ensure every cookie tastes like home. From classic chocolate chip to exotic matcha, our cookies are made with love and the finest ingredients.

Our Cookie Menu

Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chunk

Our classic cookie filled with irresistible semi-sweet chocolate chunks and a sprinkle of flaky sea salt.

Ingredients: Butter, brown sugar, eggs, plain flour, semi-sweet chocolate chunks, flaky sea salt, vanilla extract, baking soda

Double Chocolate Bliss

For chocolate lovers everywhere. Rich cocoa dough loaded with premium chocolate chips for an indulgent experience.

Ingredients: Dutch-process cocoa, dark chocolate chips, butter, caster sugar, eggs, plain flour, vanilla extract, baking soda

Biscoff Caramel Dream

Creamy Biscoff spread swirled into buttery dough for a warm, toasty, caramel-like flavor in every bite.

Ingredients: Biscoff spread, butter, brown sugar, plain flour, eggs, ground cinnamon, vanilla extract, baking soda

Red Velvet Romance

Elegant red velvet with a hint of cocoa, topped with cream cheese swirls for a sophisticated sweet treat.

Ingredients: Cocoa powder, red food colouring, cream cheese swirl, butter, caster sugar, plain flour, eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda

Matcha Green Serenity

Silky matcha powder blended into buttery dough with white chocolate chips for a unique, earthy-sweet flavor.

Ingredients: Ceremonial-grade matcha, white chocolate chips, butter, caster sugar, plain flour, eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda

Oreo Crunch Explosion

Crushed Oreos mixed into the dough and layered throughout for an iconic chocolate-vanilla cookie experience.

Ingredients: Crushed Oreos, cream filling pieces, butter, caster sugar, plain flour, eggs, vanilla extract, baking soda

Chocolate Mousse Luxury

Rich dark chocolate mousse folded into our signature cookie base for an ultra-decadent melt-in-mouth experience.

Ingredients: Dark chocolate mousse, Dutch-process cocoa, butter, heavy cream, caster sugar, plain flour, eggs, vanilla extract

Pecan Pie Paradise

Buttery dough loaded with toasted pecan pieces, reminiscent of your favorite slice of pecan pie.

Ingredients: Toasted pecans, brown sugar, butter, plain flour, eggs, pure maple syrup, ground cinnamon, vanilla extract, baking soda
